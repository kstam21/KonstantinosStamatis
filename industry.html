Industry Engagement

Bridging academia and industry.

As an officer of SPE: Inspiring Plastics Professionals at Texas A&M and Industry Co-Chair for the Student Leadership Council at the NSF HAND Engineering Research Center, I organize activities that connect students directly with industry R&D professionals — from hosting invited speakers on campus to visiting corporate research sites.

Mar 20, 2026 · College Station, Texas

Industry Seminar: From PhD to Industry — Kaneka North America

As President of SPE: Inspiring Plastics Professionals at Texas A&M, I invited Dr. Yangwei Liu (Kaneka North America) to speak, with the goal of helping students learn about industry pathways and become competitive candidates for careers at companies like Kaneka. I presented him a certificate of appreciation following his talk.

Co-hosted by SPE & HAND ERC Student Chapters · Supported by the Department of Materials Science & Engineering, Texas A&M University
