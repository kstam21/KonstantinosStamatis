Material Advantage Congressional Visits Day

Representing Texas A&M University, I met directly with Texas Senators and Representatives on Capitol Hill to advocate for increased federal investment in the physical sciences and engineering.

I emphasized the critical role materials research plays in national competitiveness — carrying the message that sustained funding for fundamental research is essential for America’s technological future. Awarded a $320 travel grant.
