Professional Profile
Hello! I'm Konstantinos.
PhD Candidate · Texas A&M SPE President BS Valedictorian #1 Nationally in Chemistry

I am a materials scientist with over five years of research experience in the United States and Greece, driven by a passion for translating fundamental science into real-world impact. My primary focus is developing polymeric actuators for soft robotic hands and artificial muscles, using Liquid Crystal Elastomers with Dynamic Covalent Bonds.

Beyond the lab, I am a dedicated leader, mentor, and public advocate — founding student organizations, teaching 80+ undergraduates, and representing the materials science community on Capitol Hill in Washington, D.C.
